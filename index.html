About ->

My name is Charles, I am an interaction designer. I am most interested in the functional intersection of science and art. I am a Masters Student at Carnegie Mellon's Graduate School of Computer Science working towards my degree in Human-Computer-Interaction. Prior to my graduate studies I practiced computational-design in Los Angeles at the Yazdani Studio R&D and received my undergraduate degree in design from the University of Colorado, Boulder. I love to build things with computers and have authored work spanning various disciplines such as software, architecture, industrial-design, graphic-design, interactive-art and digital-fabrication. My current focus is centered around the MHCI capstone project, my startup archdata.org and the production of computational-art for friends. If interested in services, collaborations or questions about my work please contact me through one of my feeds below.
