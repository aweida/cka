Stadium Seating Tool

A tool I built for the Cannon Design sports architecture group in Vancouver aids in the design and analysis for optimized stadium seating. The tool calculates numerous dimensions, distance vectors and sightlines while providing parameters to control all the elements of seating location. This allowed the architects to run through a series of design options and calculations in days, a process which typically would take several weeks, as well as provides the ability to adjust the hierarchy of parameters as the design progresses to respond to issues of geometry, economics, and circulation. The tool also produces analytical data within a customizable graphical output allowing one to visualize the information and convey the design intent to potential clients.
